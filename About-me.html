About me


I specialize in  mass spectrometry, biochemical mechanisms, and computational biochemistry. With a PhD in Clinical Biochemistry and over 20 years of research experience, I have developed a unique expertise that bridges fundamental biochemistry, drug metabolism, and advanced analytical techniques. My career includes impactful contributions to understanding adverse drug reactions, neurodegenerative diseases, and biochemical pathway analysis using integrative omics approaches, with applications in precision medicine and diagnostics. Dedicated to education, I teaches courses on clinical biochemistry, bioinformatics, and molecular drug design, preparing students to tackle modern challenges in biotechnology and health sciences.
